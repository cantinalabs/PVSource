# Resources & External Links

A curated directory of the outside resources this reference draws on and the tools, databases and agencies worth bookmarking. Everything here is an external site — always confirm current programs, prices and code editions at the source.

> **Tip:** For anything incentive- or policy-related, start with **[DSIRE](https://www.dsireusa.org/)** — the authoritative, regularly updated database of U.S. state, federal, local and utility incentives.

## State energy offices & regulators

Each state's **energy office** and **public utility commission (PUC/PSC)** administer or oversee net metering, interconnection and many incentives. Find yours via:

- [DSIRE — browse by state](https://programs.dsireusa.org/system/program) — every state's programs in one place
- [NASEO — State Energy Offices directory](https://www.naseo.org/members-states)
- [NARUC — utility commissions directory](https://www.naruc.org/about-naruc/regulatory-commissions/)
- Your **utility's** website — search "[your utility] solar / net metering / interconnection" for the authoritative local rules and any utility rebates.

For state-by-state snapshots and the states with dedicated guides on this site, see the **U.S. State Solar Map** and the **State Solar Guides** section.
